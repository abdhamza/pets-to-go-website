About Us

Mission

Founded in 2009, Pets To Go is a non-profit and Springfieldâ€™s only no-kill animal shelter.

Pets to Go is a no-kill animal shelter with a mission to provide a safe haven for homeless pets while promoting animal welfare and responsible pet ownership. Our organization is committed to creating a world where no animal is left behind or forgotten. We believe that every pet deserves a loving home and a second chance at life.

Our shelter's primary goal is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome as many pets as possible. We work tirelessly to provide a safe and comfortable environment for our furry residents while they wait for their forever homes. Our team of dedicated staff and volunteers ensure that each animal receives proper care, including medical attention, socialization, and training, so they are healthy and ready for adoption.

In addition to our adoption services, Pets to Go is also dedicated to promoting animal welfare and responsible pet ownership. We offer a variety of educational resources to the community, including pet care tips, behavior training, and spay/neuter initiatives. We believe that responsible pet ownership is the key to reducing the number of homeless pets in our community, and we are committed to doing our part.

At Pets to Go, we are also committed to building strong relationships with our adopters and the community at large. We believe that pets are more than just animals; they are beloved members of the family. That's why we work closely with our adopters to ensure a smooth transition for both the pet and their new family. We also regularly participate in community events, such as adoption fairs and fundraisers, to raise awareness about our mission and promote animal welfare.

We are a community of animal lovers who are committed to making a difference in the lives of homeless pets. Our mission is to provide hope, healing, and a second chance at life for every animal that comes through our doors. We believe that by working together, we can create a better world for animals and people alike.

As a non-profit organization, we rely heavily on donations to provide food, shelter, medical care, and other necessary resources for our furry residents. Every donation makes a significant impact on the lives of the pets we serve. By supporting you are helping us give these animals a second chance at life and a loving forever home. We kindly ask for your support to continue our mission and make a difference in the lives of these deserving pets.
